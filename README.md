# Breaking Bad

**Breaking Bad** is an American crime drama television series created by *Vince Gilligan*. It originally aired on **AMC** from 2008 to 2013.

## 🧪 Plot Summary

> “Say my name.”  
> — Walter White

The story follows **Walter White**, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer, and his former student **Jesse Pinkman**, as they descend into the world of crime.

## 🎭 Main Characters

- **Walter White** — Played by *Bryan Cranston*
- **Jesse Pinkman** — Played by *Aaron Paul*
- **Skyler White** — Played by *Anna Gunn*
- **Saul Goodman** — Played by *Bob Odenkirk*

## 🔥 My Favorite Things About the Series

1. Complex character development
2. Intense plot twists
3. Powerful cinematography
4. Brilliant acting performances

## ⭐ Rating

**9.5/10** – A must-watch for fans of crime dramas and character-driven stories.
